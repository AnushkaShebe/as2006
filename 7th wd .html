Tribute to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

"Missile Man of India" & Former President

About

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Known as the "Missile Man of India" for his work on the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology, he was a beloved leader and an inspiration to millions.

Achievements

  • Played a pivotal role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998.
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna, Padma Bhushan, and Padma Vibhushan.
  • Authored several inspirational books including "Wings of Fire".
